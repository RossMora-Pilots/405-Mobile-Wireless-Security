# Executive Summary — Bluegreen Media Wireless Security Assessment

## Situation Overview

Bluegreen Media is a 60-employee social media startup operating from a 20,000-square-foot facility with 10 wireless access points, a BYOD workforce, and five traveling account representatives. As the company evaluates an IPO, its current wireless and mobile security posture — which lacks network access control, mobile device management, and wireless intrusion monitoring — presents material risk to investor confidence, regulatory compliance, and customer data protection. An independent assessment identified critical gaps that, left unaddressed, expose the company to an estimated $173,500 in annualized loss expectancy from wireless and mobile threats.

## Key Findings

| Area | Assessment |
|---|---|
| **Current Gaps** | No NAC (unauthorized devices can join the network) · No MDM (BYOD devices unmanaged) · No WIPS (wireless attacks undetected) · Only 1 of 7 kill chain phases monitored |
| **Risk Level** | **Critical** — 4 of 12 identified threats scored Critical (risk score 20–25); estimated dwell time for wireless attacks is ~72 hours with current controls |
| **Recommended Actions** | Deploy NAC for device authentication · Implement MDM with Zero Trust conditional access · Install WIPS for continuous wireless monitoring |

## Investment Summary

| Recommendation | Year 1 Cost Range | Annual Recurring |
|---|---|---|
| Network Access Control (802.1X NAC) | $62,000–$145,000 | $12,000–$20,000 |
| MDM + Zero Trust Integration | $33,000–$58,000 | $13,000–$19,000 |
| Wireless Intrusion Prevention (WIPS) | $39,000–$101,000 | $18,000–$48,000 |
| **Combined Total** | **$134,000–$304,000** | **$43,000–$87,000** |

## Expected Outcomes

- **94% reduction in attack dwell time** — from ~72 hours to ~4 hours, converting potential material disclosure events into contained security incidents
- **100% device visibility and 7/7 kill chain phase coverage** — eliminating the current blind spots across reconnaissance, delivery, exploitation, installation, and command-and-control phases
- **SOC 2 and SOX audit readiness** — all three controls generate machine-readable compliance evidence mapped to Trust Service Criteria CC6.1–CC7.2 and SOX IT General Controls

## Timeline

All three recommendations deploy in parallel over a 20-week implementation window (July–November 2025), with NAC and WIPS completing in 16 weeks and MDM + Zero Trust completing in 20 weeks.
